# Privacy Policy — DecibelPro

**Last updated:** March 22, 2026

## Data Collection

DecibelPro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Microphone

The microphone is used exclusively to measure ambient sound levels in real time. Audio is processed locally on your device and never recorded or transmitted.

## Location

GPS location is used optionally in Dispute Mode (Mode Litige) to include coordinates in locally generated PDF reports. Location data is never transmitted outside your device.

## Local Storage

Measurement sessions are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. No data is shared with third parties.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. DecibelPro does not have access to your payment information.
